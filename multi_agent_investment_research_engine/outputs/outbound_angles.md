# Outbound Angles

Same signal evidence the investment memo used, re-framed for a sales / GTM motion. The trigger is the specific event, the persona is who you'd reach, and the opener / follow-up are concrete starting points - not finished copy.

## CRWD — CrowdStrike Holdings

**Confidence:** high  
**Trigger signal:** partnership — CRWD misses revenue estimates, shares drop

**Persona:** Head of BD

**Pain hypothesis:** CrowdStrike Holdings is taking on integration risk and looking for ways to make the partnership pay off fast.

**Opener:**

> Saw the partnership signal on CrowdStrike Holdings — "CRWD misses revenue estimates, shares drop". When that lands, the head of bd usually has 60-90 days to show measurable progress. Worth a 15-minute look at how peers handled it?

**Follow-up:**

> No worries if timing's off. We tracked three other partnership events at comparable companies in the last 90 days - happy to share the playbooks that worked.
